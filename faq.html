Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about water conservation and our platform.

How do I calculate water savings?
You can enter your daily habits like shorter showers, dishwasher usage, and garden watering in the Water Savings Tracker to see your impact.
What is rooftop rainwater harvesting?
Rooftop rainwater harvesting collects and stores rainwater from roofs for later use, reducing dependency on groundwater.
How do I know if my area is suitable for rainwater harvesting?
Our Feasibility & Assessment tool provides suggestions based on your roof area, location, rainfall, and available space.
Can I reuse greywater from sinks and showers?
Yes! Greywater can be collected and reused for gardening and flushing toilets, helping you save fresh water.
Is there any cost associated with using your platform?
No, our platform is completely free and focused on educating and empowering communities to conserve water.
