Rooftop Rainwater Harvesting Tips

Check Your Roof

Ensure your rooftop is suitable for rainwater collection. Keep it clean and free of debris to maximize water quality.

Maintain Gutters & Pipes

Regularly clean and inspect gutters, downspouts, and storage tanks to prevent blockages and contamination.

Collect Rainwater Efficiently

Install tanks or barrels to capture rainwater from your rooftop. Use first-flush diverters to remove dust and dirt from the initial rainfall.

Use Water Wisely

Use collected rainwater for gardening, flushing toilets, cleaning, or other non-drinking purposes.

Recharge Groundwater

If space allows, direct some rainwater to recharge pits, trenches, or soakaways to help replenish groundwater.

Monitor & Spread Awareness

Check water storage levels, clean tanks periodically, repair leaks immediately, and encourage your community to adopt rainwater harvesting.
