Our Story

We are a group of six passionate B.Tech CSE second-year students who came together with a shared vision – to make a real difference in the world. As we learned more about the challenges surrounding water scarcity, we realized that millions of people struggle every day just to access clean and safe water. This inspired us to take action.

Our project, BlueSprout, was born out of curiosity, responsibility, and a desire to empower communities. We wanted to combine technology, education, and practical solutions so that anyone – whether at home, in their garden, or in their neighborhood – could make a measurable impact on water conservation.

Through this platform, users can estimate rooftop rainwater harvesting feasibility, track their daily water savings, and discover smart ways to reduce wastage. Our goal is not only to save water but also to raise awareness, inspire action, and cultivate a culture of responsible water usage.

For us, this project is more than a technical assignment – it’s a mission. Every line of code, every design choice, and every feature was crafted with the hope that small actions can lead to big changes. Together, as a team of six, we aim to create a sustainable impact and motivate others to join us in conserving one of the planet’s most precious resources.

This is our story, and this is our commitment: to turn awareness into action and technology into a force for good.
